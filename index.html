Hey, I'm Cynthia Zanoni. I believe technology is one of the most powerful ways to create opportunities.

For the past 16+ years, I've been building software, supporting developers, and creating communities. Today, I'm a Global Cloud Advocate at Microsoft, focused on AI and developer tools, and the Founder of WoMakersCode, a nonprofit that has helped more than 450,000 women across 30+ countries start and grow careers in technology.

Bio

This is made for journalists (look at her!), podcast hosts, and event organizers to copy-and-paste.

Cynthia Zanoni is a Global Cloud Advocate at Microsoft, where she connects developers to the technologies shaping the future of software engineering through AI, GitHub, and Visual Studio Code.

With more than 16 years of experience in technology, she is the founder of WoMakersCode, a nonprofit organization that has already impacted more than 450,000 women across 30+ countries through free programs for education, training, and employability in technology.

In 2024, she was recognized by the UN Global Compact in Brazil as one of the most prominent leaders in advancing gender and racial equity in the technology sector.
